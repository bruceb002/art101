About Me

Hi there! My name is Bruce Bai. I am a Junior at UC Santa Cruz majoring in Computer Science. In my free time, I love singing, drawing, playing badminton, doing digital design, and even programming from time to time. My favorite singer is Mariah Carey.

Anyone who is close to me will even acknowledge that being a fan of Mariah is my entire personality.

For badminton, I was on my high school's varsity team for all four years. I did enter some competitions, and have won a couple awards. I wouldn't say I'm one of the best players at all though. For the most part, I just play for fun at the end of the day.

Speaking of competitions, I have won awards for my other endeavors as well: I won first place for a coding competition, third place for a singing competition, and even multiple awards for kung fu from when I was a kid.

Fun fact about me: as I adore Mariah Carey, I have also come to learn how to sing her special whistle notes.

Anywho, thanks for sticking around for my introduction. Have fun exploring the rest of this page!
